"The Battle of Salania"

planets. The Salans lived happily and calmly when suddenly the Zogals attacked. The Salans tried
to resist but couldn't and the Zogul Empire took over their home planet of Salania.
Although they are looking for a new planet, they are coming back for revenge ...
