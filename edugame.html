A brief about my EduGame

The name of my EduGame is called MathaMind. This edugame allows one to practice their Mathamatical skills
for when they are in 4th or 5th grade. This allows them to learn and practice advanced maths compared to their
age group so that they don't face any problems during the upcoming years. My game has practice
questions related to topics such as fractions and decimals and integers.
